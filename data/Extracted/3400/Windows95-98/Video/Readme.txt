Stylistic 3400 Video Drivers for Windows 98

19 May 2000

This archive contains the drivers for the Stylistic 3400 video
controller. They can be used to to install support for the video
controller if no drivers are present in your Windows 98 installation.

For additional instructions on how to install these drivers, please
contact your local help desk or reseller.  You can also contact your
assigned FPSI Systems Engineer or call FPSI Customer Support at one of
the numbers listed in our Web site at
<http://www.fpsi.fujitsu.com/support/>.
