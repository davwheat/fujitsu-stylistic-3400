Stylistic 3400 Modem Drivers for Windows NT

15 June 2000

This archive contains the drivers for the Stylistic 3400 internal modem.
They can be used to to install support for the modem if no drivers are
present in your Windows NT installation.

For detailed instructions on how to install these drivers, please
contact your local help desk or reseller.  You can also contact your
assigned FPC Systems Engineer or call FPC Customer Support at one of
the numbers listed in our Web site at
<http://www.fpsi.fujitsu.com/support/>.
